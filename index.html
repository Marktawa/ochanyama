About Us

Ocha Nyama by Marlin is a vibrant and cozy pub and grill located in the heart of Lusaka, Zambia, at East Park Mall. We take pride in offering a unique dining experience that blends delicious food, refreshing drinks, and a warm ambiance.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Ocha Nyama by Marlin is the go-to destination for food enthusiasts and friends seeking great moments. Our journey started with a passion for creating exceptional dishes that celebrate Zambian and international flavors.

Cuisine and Specialties

At Ocha Nyama by Marlin, we specialize in mouthwatering grilled dishes, succulent barbecues, and a variety of classic pub favorites. Our menu is carefully crafted using locally sourced, fresh ingredients to ensure a delightful and satisfying experience for every guest.

Our Team

Our culinary team, is dedicated to delivering culinary excellence with every plate. With a passion for culinary innovation, our team strives to create memorable flavors that will keep you coming back for more.

Visit Us

We invite you to visit Ocha Nyama by Marlin at East Park Mall, where good food meets good times. Whether you're looking to unwind after work, celebrate a special occasion, or simply enjoy a delightful meal with friends and family, our welcoming staff is ready to make your visit exceptional.
